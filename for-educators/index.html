We start differently

We teach what AI gets wrong before we teach how to use it.

Most AI literacy curricula start with the tools. Here's ChatGPT. Here's how to prompt it. That's useful — but it serves the interests of the platforms, not your students. Climer starts with the questions that cautious educators are already asking.

What we don't do

In plain language, for the skeptical educator.

  • We don't tell students AI is automatically good.
  • We don't encourage uncritical AI use.
  • We don't take money from the AI platforms we teach about. (We're grant funded.)
  • We don't collect or sell student data.
  • We don't have a paid tier that creates equity issues in your classroom.
  • We don't gatekeep any lesson content.
